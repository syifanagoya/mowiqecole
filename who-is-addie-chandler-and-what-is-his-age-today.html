Who is Addie Chandler and What Is His Age Today?

 Posted on February 29, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1111 words  |  Martina Birk

Addie Chandler is an American voice actor born in Los Angeles, California, United States of America, who has lent his vocal abilities to the production of movies such as American Horror Story (2011) and Colin in Black & White (2021). He recently made a lot of waves as the voice behind the character, Devon, in Turning Red (2022). Addie Chandler’s current age is very uncertain as he has not made his date of birth public yet, however, it is widely assumed that he was born between 2004 and 2007.

How Old Is Addie Chandler?

Based on that assumption, Addie Chandler could be either 19 or 16 years old at this time. He is an American citizen, having been born in the United States of America, and currently lives in Los Angeles, California with his family but he has not explicitly revealed his exact place of birth.

Addie Chandler Began Acting Before He Turned 10 Years Old

The mystery surrounding the young American voice actor’s date of birth means that it is almost impossible to determine the exact age at which he began acting. Without ruling out the possibility of that very important detail changing and working with the assumption that he was born between 2004 and 2007, it is safe to assume that Addie Chandler was around 6 and 9 years old when he officially made his screen debut in 2013.

Whichever may be the case, the fact that he was most definitely less than 10 years old at the time is a credit to the actor’s abilities at such a very young age. His first official credit came in the TV series titled Outside The Box as Danny in the episode titled The Shining.

Three years after his debut, Addie followed his performance in Outside The Box with a resounding voicing of the character Otto/Notto Prince in the 2016 short movie Barbie: Dreamtopia. The following year he reprised his voice role in the short movie’s sequel, Barbie Dreamtopia: Festival of Fun.

He is Most Famous For Voicing Three Characters in Harvey Beaks

It is a well-known fact that every actor or actress has that one role that marks the turning point of his or her career. For Addie Chandler, that aura of prominence hit when he voiced the characters Rooter, Roger, and Elves in Greenblatt’s animated series Harvey Beaks. This all happened over 6 episodes of the series between 2016 to 2017.

From 2017 to 2018, Addie returned to voice what has become an iconic character for him, Otto/Notto Prince in the TV series of Barbie Dreamtopia. Over the years, Addie Chandler has appeared in several other movies and TV series, including Colin in Black and White, The Loud House, American Horror Story, We Bare Bears, and most recently in the just-released Turning Red.
