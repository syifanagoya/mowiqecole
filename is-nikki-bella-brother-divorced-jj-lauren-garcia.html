Is Nikki Bella brother divorced? Here is more about JJ Garcia and Lauren Garcia’s married life and children.

Is Nikki Bella Brother Divorced? JJ And Lauren Garcia Separated

Nikki Bella’s brother JJ Garcia has already divorced his wife, Lauren Garcia.

JJ and Lauren are the co-parents of two daughters, Vivienne and Alice. 

However, after ten years of marriage, the couple announced their separation in July 2021. 

On July 20, 2021, Lauren announced on her Instagram that she had decided to separate from her husband, JJ Garcia.

She wrote that there had been a shift in their lives in the past couple of months.

Despite efforts by the Garcia couple trying to navigate the problem, they could not find any reason to continue their marriage, implying that separation was the best for both of them.

Lauren asked for privacy and respect and did not want to reveal further about her separation from JJ.

The Bella Twins’s brother has not spoken publicly on the matter.

More About Nikki Bella Brother JJ Garcia

JJ Garcia is the younger brother of former WWE wrestlers duo Bella Twins, Nikki and Brie Bella.

Jonathan James Garcia was born in San Diego, California, on March 31, 1986. He was raised in the Phoenix suburb of Scottsdale, Arizona.

He is the son of Kathy Colace and Jon Garcia, who separated after the birth of their three children.

The Garcia siblings’ mother, Kathy, later married retired wrestler and business executive John Laurinaitis.  

JJ works with his mother at her company JBN & Associates.

JJ has a considerable following on social media. His Instagram, @thevacationjj, has over 209K followers.

He often posts pictures of his daughters and sisters, The Bella Twins, on his profile.

More About Lauren Garcia

Lauren Garcia is an entrepreneur and a social media influencer, famous for being the ex-wife of JJ Garcia, the Bella Twins’ brother.

Often called Lola, Lauren was born on July 24, 1986, in Syracuse, New York. 

She is a lifestyle content creator famous for being the ex-sister-in-law of the Bella Twins.

Her blog, What Lola Likes, contains content about her love of fashion, dogs, and health-conscious cooking.

Lauren has a massive social media following. She has over 354K followers on her profile, @whatlolalikes.

She frequently uploads pictures of her daughters and the products she endorses and promotes.

JJ and Lauren have appeared several times in the reality series Total Bellas.
