League of Legends Preseason 2024: When Does It Begin?

 Posted on May 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  600 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

When Does It Begin?

The excitement is building as League of Legends gears up for Preseason 2024! The testing phase kicked off on November 20, 2023, exclusively on PBE servers. But, hold your horses; the official release on regular servers might take a bit longer. Players can tweak their Season 13 ranks until January 3, 202The ongoing season might stretch a bit to accommodate changes from the test servers.

What Can Players Expect?

Riot Games is on a mission to make League of Legends even more fun and faster for players. In 2024, they’re breaking down the ranked season into three parts: January, May, and September. That means your rank gets a reset three times a year, starting with Season 1No more year-long grind! This change is designed to give you more chances to climb the ranks and snag those rewards. Get ready for shorter, more exciting splits!

And there’s more! Riot is shaking things up with in-game items. They’re bidding farewell to Mythic items introduced in 202These items were supposed to offer more choices but unintentionally put a damper on character customization. Riot’s take? Removing them brings back diversity, giving players more ways to craft and develop their characters.

League of Legends in a Nutshell

League of Legends, or LoL, is a game by Riot Games since 200It’s a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) where two teams of five players battle it out. The goal? Control the opponent’s territory and crush their “Nexus,” a super important structure in the game.

Each player controls a unique character called a “champion,” each with its own set of cool abilities. As the game goes on, players earn experience points and gold to buy items that make their champions even more awesome. And the best part? It’s free to play! But if you want to jazz up your character, there’s an option to buy cool stuff. Available on Windows and macOS, it’s a game that brings players from all over together.

League of Legends’ Popularity

League of Legends is crazy popular for good reasons. It’s easy to get into, has a ton of characters, and looks amazing. Even with its long history, it’s still one of the best games around. But, like any big game, there are some players who can be not-so-nice. Riot Games is actively working to stop this. With millions of players worldwide, League of Legends has inspired music, comics, stories, and even an animated series called “Arcane.” And there are other games connected to League of Legends, like a mobile version and a card game.

Countdown to LoL Preseason 2024

Get ready, gamers! The upcoming LoL Preseason 2024 is bringing a wave of awesome changes. Riot Games is giving us a better ranked system, cooler gameplay, and tweaks to in-game items. It’s all about making the gaming experience top-notch. So, brace yourselves for a journey filled with challenges, sweet rewards, and fierce competition in the new season.

FAQs

Q: Will my rank reset during the 2024 Preseason?

A: Nope! Unlike before, your rank won’t reset during the preseason. You’ll smoothly move from Season 13 to Season 14 with just a one-week break.

Q: When’s the official start date for LoL Preseason 2024?

A: Circle January 9, 2024, on your calendars! That’s when LoL Preseason 2024 officially begins, alongside Patch 14.1.

Q: What changes are coming to in-game items?

A: Riot Games is saying goodbye to Mythic items. Introduced in 2021 to give more options, they ended up limiting character creativity. Removing them brings back diversity in character building. Get ready for a fresh take in the new season!
